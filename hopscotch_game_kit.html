WHAT IS THIS GAME?

This is a fun hopscotch game where you decode secret words!

CODING = Hiding a message using symbols (like ‚≠ê ‚ù§Ô∏è üîµ)
DECODING = Reading the symbols to find the real word

You don't need a computer - just jump and decode!

WHAT YOU NEED:

  • Colored chalk (for drawing on playground)
  • This decoder card sheet (cut the cards below)
  • A small stone or beanbag (for hopscotch)
  • Friends to play with!

HOW TO PLAY:

  • STEP 1: Draw hopscotch boxes on the ground with chalk
  • STEP 2: Pick a secret word (like CAT or RAIN)
  • STEP 3: Put symbol cards in the boxes to spell your word
  • STEP 4: Jump through the boxes and remember the symbols
  • STEP 5: Use your decoder cards to find the letters
  • STEP 6: Say the word - You decoded the secret message! üéâ
